Meet Michelle

Michelle’s Kitchen is a cooking class focused on fun, hands-on meal preparation where the guest learns the art of cuisine and enjoys a two course meal together.

An intimate group of three to six people is led by Michelle as she teaches California-style cuisine, based on French techniques.

Michelle’s Kitchen is a unique experience in a historic residence, where guests are treated to a culinary education that includes a gourmet meal paired with wine.

Michelle’s love of cooking led her to Le Cordon Bleu, College of Culinary Arts. The joy of teaching started her cooking classes in 2011.
